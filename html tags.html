3. Article tag indicate a self contained piece of content , such as a blog post or news article .
4. Section tag represent a thematic grouping of a content on a web page .
5. Aside tag is typicaly used for side bars or content that is tangentially related to the main content.
6. Footer tag represent the footer of the web page , usually containing a copyright information and contact details.
9. Main tag is signifies a main content of a web page .
